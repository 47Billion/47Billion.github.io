Architecture Definition

We specialize in providing architecture solutions for new products. We help start-ups as well as small and medium scale organizations define and implement end-to-end scalable and high-availability product architecture based on their road-map. We work with you to understand scalability, availability, budget and timeline requirements and provide detail architecture using open source products and cloud services . We provide phased rollout plan starting with POC to the deployment.

Process Advisory

We also provide consultancy in defining phased rollout of product, work with you to understand your customer and pivot your pilot, and put together development methodology and tools for multi-site agile development and automation.

User Experience and Design

Our UX process and best practices provide the basis for designing software that is intuitive and aesthetically pleasing for your users. We employ proven and objective data-gathering and analysis techniques to develop a clear understanding of who the users are and how they will use the product. We help you understand your end-user personas, perform usability studies, provide interactive high-fidelity mockups that can be tested before developing production applications saving significant time and money on re-iterations after development.

Product Development & Testing

Our team has development experience in variety of languages, technologies and frameworks like Java, C#, PHP, HTML5, JQuery, Angular, Backbone, d3js, Spring, .NET, Solr, Mahout, Kafka, RabbitMQ, MySQL, Oracle, SQL Server, Hadoop, HDFS, Cassandra, Redis, MongoDB, Android and iOS development.

Our quality assurance team uses state-of-the-art automation tools to provide high-quality testing services. We work very closely with development teams to make sure right tests are developed and run from product development phase all the way to integration and deployment testing.

Performance Measurement and Optimization

Our team of senior consultants have deep experience in developing highly scalable as well as small footprint applications – some of which are currently being used by large number of organizations. We provide services to design and develop new high performance product as well as to re-architect exiting products to optimize their performance and scalability, and/or make them cloud ready.
